People Impact

I spent the summer of 2018 working at People Impact, an international educational institution, to work on creating marketing material for their company. My first project was to create various souvenirs including lanyard badges, pins, pamphlets, and more for a summit. Moreover, I created brand-new marketing material for the company to increase brand awareness. To make the designs eye-catching, I have made the designs to be simplistic, as most print advertisements in Hong Kong and China are filled with text.
