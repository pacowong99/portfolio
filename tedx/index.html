From Ashes We Rise

Creating a distinct brand for TEDxIndianaUniversity.

Professional Yet Distinctive

This design allows the audience to view this as not just a student-run conference, but it is something more. The neon lights provides a distinctive design for the audience to recognize.

Created for Different Purposes

When I created this design, I envisioned it to be used for different purposes, like lanyard badges, tickets, speaker announcements, and so on. The goal is to provide a consistent experience for the conference audience.

Result of a Collaboration

Everything in TEDxIndianaUniversity is about teamwork, and this is no exception. The Production team meets regularly to critique designs. We also work with other teams to provide them with the graphics they needed.

Iterated Until Perfection

I created multiple designs and iterations before I got to the final design, where I am finally satisfied with the design. This is one of the older iterations of this design.
